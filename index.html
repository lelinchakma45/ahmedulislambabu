About Me

AKM Ahmedul Islam is the Founder of TechKnowGram Limited and now serving the company as Managing Director & CEO. His 25+ years of professional experience in working in Japan market BPO, outsourcing, and software industry have enabled him to be one of the pioneers in the ICT Industry. From the inception of his professional career, Mr. Ahmed served several multinational companies (NIIT, BJIT, ServicEngineBPO, and AtomAP) and successfully secured Key positions. He has been serving as a chair, co-chair, and member of a number of standing committees of BASIS since 2001 and also at Bangladesh Computer Samity BCS, Bangladesh Association of Call Center & Outsourcing BACCO , Japan Bangladesh Chamber of Commerce and Industries JBCCI and E-Commerce Association of Bangladesh eCAB. Ahmed leads the participation of a 78 member ICT business team in Japan IT Week (spring) 2016 at Big Sight in Tokyo as the Convener of Japan Focus Group at BASIS.

He also represents Bangladesh in many national and international ICT events and forums. Ahmed is the Director of the Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services BASIS. Also serving as Consultant at JICA Bangladesh: : as Chairman of BASIS & BACCO Japan Desk Ahmed was a prominent ICT journalist and technology writer. He served as an executive editor of the Monthly Computer Barta and senior correspondent of the Bangladesh Observer.

He is one of the founders and founder president of the Bangladesh ICT Journalist Forum (BIJF). He has both Bachelor and Master Degree in Applied Physics Electronics and Communication Engineering from the University of Dhaka and an MBA degree from the Presidency University of Bangladesh and also ACMP 4.0 from IBA, University of Dhaka.
